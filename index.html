About Me

I'm a second-year master student at the Shenzhen International Graduate School of Tsinghua University (SIGS, THU) in Shenzhen, supervised by Professor Yong Jiang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree (2017-2021) from Wuhan University.

My research interests mainly include (1) Programmable data plane and its application: such as network failure localization, network function virtualization (NFV) based on programmable data plane, etc. (2) AI for network, especially graph neural network for intelligent network system.

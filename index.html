About Me

I'm a master student at the Shenzhen International Graduate School of Tsinghua University (SIGS, THU) in Shenzhen, supervised by Professor Yong Jiang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree (2017-2021) from Wuhan University. My research interests mainly include (1) Programmable Data Plane and Its Application: such as network failure localization, network function virtualization (NFV) based on programmable data plane, etc. (2) Applicable Graph Mining: explore applications of graph machine learning in recommender systems, social networks, computer networks, game match and so on.
